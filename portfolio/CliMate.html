I joined the CliMate team as a UX Research consultant & lead designer in January 2022. Our app integrates well-established therapy techniques with climate action.

Awards: 2nd place at the Big Idea Blitz (University of Pittsburgh; Feb. 2022).

Contributions: focus groups, affinity diagramming, lead design for all deliverables.

Problem: Need to address climate anxiety
1 in 3 Americans is very worried about climate change, and less than half know what to do about it. But a one-size-fits-all solution can't address the unique situations that underlie climate change and the individuals themselves; a student in rural Tennessee has different needs, resources, and eco-anxieties than a parent in urban Chicago.

Existing climate-focused apps fail to address climate anxiety & lack insights from behavior health. And anxiety-focused apps ignore the realities of climate change & lifestyle changes it requires.
