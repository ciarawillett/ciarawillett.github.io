FUTURE
I joined the FUTURE team as a UX Research consultant & lead designer in January 2022. Our app integrates well-established therapy techniques with climate action.

Awards: 2nd place at the Big Idea Blitz (University of Pittsburgh; Feb. 2022).

Research Methods: focus groups, affinity diagramming.

Problem: Need to address climate anxiety
1 in 3 Americans is very worried about climate change, and Millennials / Gen-Z are increasingly discussing climate change in therapy (NPR). But less than half of Americans know ways to combat climate change, leaving many to feel helpless.

Currently, no app simultaneously addresses climate anxiety & climate action. Climate-focused apps fail to address climate anxiety & lack insights from behavior health. And anxiety-focused apps ignore the realities of climate change & lifestyle changes it requires.
