covidBuddy
In March 2020, the US shut down due to the COVID-19 pandemic. But vulnerable populations needed help getting access to groceries, medicine, and supplies. My lab pooled our talents to develop the covidBuddy app - a minimalistic social networking tool with the sole goal of connecting volunteers with those in need.

After a week, my team had an almost finished product...and then larger companies (e.g., Nextdoor) rolled out highly similar features to their already expansive networks. Our goal was to help people, not earn a profit - so we shut down instead of over-saturating the market. But we learned a lot, and it made quarantine just a tiny bit brighter.

Contributions: prototyping, front-end developer.

Tools Used: Sketch, HTML/CSS, javascript.
